Important Forms

Routine Inspection/Entry Request Form

You will receive a version of this form when the owner or agent needs access to the property. The reasons for requesting entry are listed on the form itself.

  • Notice of Proposed Entry to Premises Form 19

Breaches (Against Tenant)

There are two breaches that a tenant can receive. A form 20, which is for reasons other than paying rent, such as untidy gardens, overdue bills and invoices, or disturbing neighborus. A form 21 is solely for rent arrears.

  • Breach to Tenant Form 20
  • Breach to Tenant Form 21

Breaches (Against Owner)

Tenants are able to breach an owner if they believe the owner isn't complying with the lease contract. This form is generally used when the owner is not performing urgent and necessary maintenance.

  • Breach to Owner Form 23

Termination Forms

There are three types of terminations: a form 1A is for non-payment of rent and is issued after a breach form 20. A form 1B is for rent arrears as well, but doesn't require a breach form. A form 1C is for reasons other than rent arrears, and the potential reasons are listed on the form itself.

  • Termination Form 1A
  • Termination Form 1B
  • Termination Form 1C
